Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2025

Summary: Energy Atlas respects your privacy. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device.

1. Data Collection

Energy Atlas does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely offline using data included within the application.

2. Energy Data

The app displays publicly available energy consumption and greenhouse gas emission data from the World Bank and other public sources. This data is:

  • Included in the app at download
  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to external servers

3. Apple Intelligence Features

Energy Atlas uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework to provide AI-powered insights. All AI processing occurs locally on your Apple Vision Pro:

  • No prompts or responses are sent to external servers
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • All analysis happens on-device using Apple Intelligence

4. Location Services

The app does not access or use your device's location services.

5. Analytics

The app does not use any analytics services or tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

Energy Atlas is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
